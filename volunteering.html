COVID-19 Vaccination Drives

Education & Mentorship
Academic Mentor for High School Students

Arts & Cultural Outreach
Summer Dance Workshops — Jazz, Ballet & Contemporary
Community & Nupoor Zankar Bharatnatyam Institute  ·  Mumbai

Led summer workshops in the community and at the Nupoor Zankar Bharatnatyam Institute to introduce children and young adults to jazz, ballet, and contemporary dance. 2019 – 2022
Healthcare & Arts Therapy
Dance & Yoga Workshops for Pediatric Cancer Patients
Tata Memorial Centre  ·  Mumbai

Taught dance and yoga to children receiving treatment at Tata Memorial Centre, one of India's leading cancer hospitals. Conducted skill-building and recreational workshops designed to promote emotional well-being, stress relief, and moments of joy for pediatric patients and their families during a deeply challenging time.

2019 – 2020
Civic Initiative

Spark A Smile — Founder

Founded a civic outreach program in Mumbai in 2021, organizing citywide clothing drives and COVID relief kits. Distributed 2,000+ clothing items and care packages to 150+ families affected by the pandemic. The initiative was awarded "Best Social Service Provider during COVID-19" by the Nationalist Youth Congress, Mumbai. (Mar 2021 – May 2022)
