Somatic Studies · Menstrual Wellness 2024
WombWisdom: A Somatic Healing Map for Menstrual Wellness and PCOS

A visual and conceptual map exploring the relationship between yoga, somatic healing, and menstrual wellness centered on PCOS. Blending endocrine awareness, breathwork, organ-based embodiment, and culturally rooted healing practices, the project reframes healing as a process of regulation, rhythm, and reconnection.

Somatic Psychology · Yoga Studies 2023
Somatic Observation and Recovery Through Yoga

An exploration of yoga as a method of somatic observation, self-regulation, and recovery. Rooted in Indian yogic and Ayurvedic traditions, the project documents a two-week studio practice centered on breathwork, pelvic release, and embodied listening — investigating how breath, gravity, vibration, and repetition can restore homeostasis and deepen somatic literacy.

Clinical Psychology · Arts Therapy 2024
Mental Health Stigma and Creative Arts Therapy

An examination of whether arts-based interventions can reduce public stigma around mental illness more effectively than information-only education. Drawing on dual-process theories of thinking, the paper argues that stigma operates as a fast, intuitive judgment — and that creative arts interventions are most effective when they disrupt expectation, humanize lived experience, and are paired with guided discussion and psychoeducation.
