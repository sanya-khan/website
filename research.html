International Publication

Peer-Reviewed Poster · Manuscript in Preparation

Health-Related Quality of Life in Pediatric Autoinflammatory Disease — India's Pioneering HRQoL Study Using KIDSCREEN-52

Poster presented at the 13th ISSAID International Conference  ·  Paris, France  ·  2025  ·  Manuscript in preparation for international submission

Authored India's pioneering study applying the KIDSCREEN-52 instrument to assess health-related quality of life in children with autoinflammatory diseases. The study addresses a critical gap in pediatric rare disease research in India, where no prior HRQoL data existed for this population.

Interviewed 20+ bilingual caregivers at SRCC Children's Hospital, Mumbai, analyzing psychosocial data across 10 domains to identify disparities in healthcare access. Calculated and synthesized quality-of-life scores, generating pilot trend statistics that informed research directions. The work was presented as a poster at ISSAID 2025 in Paris, with the manuscript now in preparation for international submission.
